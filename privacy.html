Varnish & Veil Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

Varnish & Veil is published by SPRING DATA LIMITED. The app is an offline iPhone and iPad puzzle game with no account system, no advertising, no analytics SDK, and no tracking.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data in Varnish & Veil. The app does not ask for your name, email address, location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or account details.

Local Game Progress

The app stores restoration progress locally on your device using Apple system storage. This progress is not sent to SPRING DATA LIMITED.

Purchases

Varnish & Veil offers a one-time non-consumable In-App Purchase called Full Atelier Unlock. Purchase processing, receipts, refunds, and restore behaviour are handled by Apple through StoreKit and your Apple ID.

Tracking and Advertising

The app does not track you across apps or websites. The app contains no third-party advertising and no third-party analytics SDK.

Children

Varnish & Veil is designed as a general audience puzzle game and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because the app does not send personal data to us, there is no server-side account data for SPRING DATA LIMITED to delete. You can delete local game progress by deleting the app from your device.

Changes

We may update this policy if the app's features or data practices change. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@springdata.uk.
