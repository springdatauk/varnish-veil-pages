Common Questions

Does Varnish & Veil require an account?
No. The app has no account system and no backend service.

Is the Full Atelier Unlock a subscription?
No. It is a one-time non-consumable purchase. There is no trial and no auto-renewal.

How do I restore a purchase?
Open the Unlock tab in the app and use Restore Purchases while signed in with the Apple ID used for the purchase.

Where is progress saved?
Progress is saved locally on your device.
